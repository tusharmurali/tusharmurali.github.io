Solutions to Zill and Shanahan's A First Course in Complex Analysis

1.1 Complex Numbers and Their Properties

1. \(i^8 = (i^4)^2 = (1)^2 = 1\).

2. \(i^{11} = (i^4)^2 \cdot i^3 = (1)^2 \cdot i^3 = -i\).

3. \(i^{42} = (i^4)^{10} \cdot i^2 = (1)^{10} \cdot i^2 = -1\).

4. \(i^{105} = (i^4)^{26} \cdot i = (1)^{26} \cdot i = i\).
